TelegramTransport is a custom pipecat FrameProcessor that bridges pytgcalls with the pipeline.

Emotion picker sets Cartesia emotion dynamically based on the text being spoken.

Key rotation for Deepgram and Cartesia keeps the bot resilient to rate limits.
